Power Verbs for Tech Resumes

Your resume is your personal pitch. Using strong action verbs immediately creates an impression of impact and ownership. Instead of writing what you were "responsible for," show what you achieved.

💥 Power Verbs for Different Resume Sections:

🔹 Coding & Development

Engineered Developed Implemented Automated Programmed Optimized Debugged Refactored Integrated Deployed

🔹 Problem Solving

Resolved Troubleshot Investigated Diagnosed Improved Simplified Streamlined

🔹 Teamwork & Collaboration

Collaborated Coordinated Contributed Assisted Mentored Partnered Liaised

🔹 Leadership & Initiative

Led Spearheaded Initiated Managed Oversaw Facilitated Delegated Directed

🔹 Analysis & Data

Analyzed Interpreted Evaluated Measured Audited Synthesized Forecasted

🔹 Results & Impact

Achieved Exceeded Increased Reduced Accelerated Generated Delivered Saved

✅ Tips to Use These Verbs Effectively:

  • ✔

    Always start bullet points with a strong action verb.

  • ✔

    Try to quantify your impact:
    Example: *Optimized frontend performance, reducing load time by 40%*

  • ✔

    Avoid passive language like “was involved in” or “responsible for”.

  • ✔

    Vary your verbs across different bullet points to keep the resume dynamic.
